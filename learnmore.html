Venture for America is a two-year fellowship program that takes top college graduates and places them in start-ups throughout the United States.

Fellows come from a diverse array of schools, majors, and experiences, all sharing a common drive to have an impact on American cities through all facets of entrepreneurship.

Once fellows move to a VFA city they gain the invaluable experience of working for a start-up and become part the VFA network in their city and across the country.
