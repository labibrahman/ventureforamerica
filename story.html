Labib Rahman


    Hometown: Portsmouth, RI
    School: Harvard University
    Major: Computer Science & Dance
    Company, City: Mason, Detroit
    Class: 2014 Fellow
    Super Vegetable: Arugula

Why VFA?

I love this question. During college I started two companies. I know what you're probably thinking: "Wow that's awesome!" Yes, while it was cool, I realized through my two failures, that building a company requires discipline, patience, and a relentlessness to persevere through difficulties. I know, that one day I want to start and run a successful business. VFA provides a path towards obtaining that goal by providing me with an opportunity to learn from successful entrepreneurs while growing a business from the ground up.

What was the Application process like?

At first I was pretty intimidated by the length of the application but then I remembered "No Pressure, No Diamonds," and really starting diving in and feeling excited about what Venture for America had to offer. Throughout the process, especially at selection day, I was really impressed by the caliber of students competing to gain a spot as a VFA Fellow.

Tell us about the Company Matchmaking...

Fellows are placed on a portal system that provides over 300 opportunities within 150 startups in 12 developing cities across the US. I had a chance to explore companies ranging from startup breweries to security businesses. Without VFA, I wouldn't have been able to be exposed to these amazing prospects.

Training Camp Highlights...

Hands down, it was staying up all night with my small group, four days into the training, programming the winning website for the first team challenge.

Your Work Experience Thus Far Has Been...

I know it sounds rather corny but I really feel that throughout my fellowship I have had a chance to make an impact in my company and my city. I have been on the ground level of development requiring me to wear a variety of different hats while facing the numerous growing pains associated with building a successful business. It's been an amazing journey thus far.

Plans Going Forward?

Based on the recent growth of Mason - adding four employees in the past three months - I plan to stick with the company through the end of my two year fellowship with VFA and for the foreseeable future.

Any recommendations for Prospective Fellow?

I want a prospective fellow to know that for me, VFA provides the perfect balance between agency and guidance. I have the ability to learn skills, develop as an entrepreneur, and impact the direction of my company all while growing my network. This is an incredible opportunity and I feel very fortunate to have been selected.
